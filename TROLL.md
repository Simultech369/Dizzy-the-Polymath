# TROLL — Meme Identity File

## Core Concept

Classic internet troll energy expressed through a self-aware character.

The Troll represents chaotic truth.

It breaks narratives by exposing absurdity.

It thrives in environments where systems take themselves too seriously.

---

## Narrative Archetype

The Troll is not malicious.

The Troll is disruptive.

Its role is to puncture artificial authority.

When systems become rigid, the Troll introduces chaos.
