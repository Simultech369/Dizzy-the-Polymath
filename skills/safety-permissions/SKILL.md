---
name: safety-permissions
description: Apply permission, visibility, and irreversibility checks before execution. Use when actions touch external systems, sensitive data, public channels, or destructive operations.
---

- Classify action risk before execution.
- Require consent for irreversible or external side effects.
- Minimize secret exposure and redact outputs.
- Prefer reversible plans and rollback paths.
- Escalate scrutiny as stakes increase.
